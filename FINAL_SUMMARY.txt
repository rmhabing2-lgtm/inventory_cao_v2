🔄 6-PHASE LIFECYCLE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  PENDING
    ↓ (Admin approves)
  APPROVED (Item Reserved)
    ↓ (Admin releases)
  RELEASED (Item Borrowed + Out)
    ↓ (Cron monitors daily)
  MONITORING (Auto-escalation on overdue)
    ↓ (Staff requests return)
  RETURN_PENDING (Awaiting inspection)
    ↓ (Admin inspects)
    ├─→ CLOSED (Path A: Good condition)
    │   └─→ Item back to Available
    │
    └─→ INCIDENT (Path C: Damaged/Lost)
        ├─→ Item status: Unserviceable
        ├─→ Incident report created
        ├─→ Financial liability recorded
        └─→ Staff/admin notifications sent
